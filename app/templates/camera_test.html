To allow camera access:

  1. Tap the lock icon in your browser's address bar
  2. Find "Camera" in the permissions list
  3. Change it to "Allow"
  4. Refresh this page

If you don't see the permission prompt, check your browser settings to ensure the site has permission to use the camera.
